Checkout Flow Optimization

Rethinking a multi-step desktop checkout into a faster, clearer path to purchase — cutting friction, drop-off, and cognitive load at the moment that matters most.

RoleProduct Designer
PlatformDesktop Web
DomainE-commerce
FocusFlow & Conversion
Overview

Removing friction from the final mile

Checkout is where intent turns into revenue — or disappears. This project audited a long, multi-screen flow and rebuilt it around clarity, fewer decisions, and confidence at every step.

01 · Problem

Too many steps

Users dropped off across a fragmented, multi-page flow with unclear progress, hidden costs, and forced account creation.

02 · Approach

Consolidate & clarify

Collapsed the flow into focused stages, surfaced totals early, added guest checkout, and made progress and trust visible throughout.

03 · Outcome

A confident path

A streamlined, predictable checkout that reduces hesitation and gets motivated buyers to confirmation with less effort.

Analytics Dashboard & Design System

A data-dense analytics dashboard backed by a scalable, token-driven design system — turning complex metrics into clear decisions while keeping the whole product consistent.

RoleProduct Designer
PlatformWeb App
DomainSaaS
FocusData Viz & Systems
Overview

Clarity at scale, from token to chart

Dashboards live or die by legibility. This project paired a thoughtfully structured analytics surface with a reusable design system, so insight stays fast to read and the interface stays consistent as the product grows.

01 · Problem

Data without direction

Metrics were scattered across inconsistent screens, making trends hard to spot and the UI costly to extend.

02 · Approach

System-first design

Built a token-driven library — color, type, spacing, components — then composed a focused dashboard with clear hierarchy and purposeful data viz.

03 · Outcome

Consistent & scalable

A legible analytics experience and a reusable system that keeps every new screen on-brand and quick to build.
